Our Story

Sanitary Workers Pakistan, An Awareness Campaign

Welcome to Sanitary Workers Pakistan, dedicated to Pakistan's unsung heroes: our sanitary workers. They tirelessly maintain our communities, yet often remain unseen and unappreciated. Join our campaign to recognize their efforts and advocate for their rights, aiming for a cleaner and more inclusive Pakistan.

Our Mission

Empowering Pakistan's unsung sanitary workers through awareness and recognition.

Founded
Volunteer Count

Zarrar Haider

Founder

I am Zarrar Haider Bhatti, hailing from a humble village background, and I proudly hold the distinction of being the first person in my village to pursue a LLB degree at the University of London.

This educational journey has illuminated my path to understand the struggles of villagers and the sanitary workers in Pakistan. I am committed to raising awareness, advocating for their rights, and striving for a more equitable society.
